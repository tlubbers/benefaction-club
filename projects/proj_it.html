BGC IT Upgrades

Status: Completed


Project Story


Our first day at BGC.


The Benefaction's very first offical meeting was held on site at The Boys and Girls Club of Bloomington-Normal. Having only just met eachother on ILSTU's campus, we were knee-deep in the forming stage of group development. The only thing that we knew, was that we didn't know much about The Boys and Girls Club. Tyler suggested we go check out the club before we did anything else.

We figured the best way to figure out how to help, was to meet who we were trying to help! After arriving at The Boys and Girls Club, it didn't take long for us to start lining up our skills with BGCGN's needs.

The computer lab upgrade project is born!


Jenny, our tour guide, was very pleased to hear that we had two computer science majors and two information technology majors. It turns out the club was in severe need of some computer nerds.

While the club had two well established computer labs, complete with over 30 workstations, a lot of the machines had problems. Apart from the workstations individual issues, many machines had not been upgraded to the clubs most recent volume licenced versions of Windows 8.1. The club also needed to implement the newest version of Microsoft Office into the labs.

A few members of The Benefaction were very familiar with upgrading/reinstalling operating systems on desktop computers, so at our very next meeting we decided to officially pursue the IT Upgrade project.

After a month of group planning and coordinating with Jenny at the club, we set the date for It Upgrade Day.

IT Upgrade Day Arrives!

Upgrade day was success! We had a lot of fun. We spent about 6 hours at the club, and when we left, every workstation was outfitted with...

  1. The newest version of Microsoft Windows 8.1

  2. The news version of Microsoft Office

  3. The proper activation key/licence.

  4. A completely refreshed and formatted hardrive.

  5. A user account for both student and administrator, with customized access privledges.

  6. All hardware issues (pardon a few without a network cable) resolved.

We had a great time at the club on IT Day. Be sure to check out the photos below! Jenny was very pleased with our work, and wrote the following email.

From Jenny Hall jhall@bgcb-n.org On Oct 29, 2014

Tyler
Thanks so much!
Your team really did an awesome job updating the computers and helping out around the Club where needed when you had downtown (waiting for the upgrades). We really appreciate all you do.

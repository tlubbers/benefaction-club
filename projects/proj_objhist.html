Project Story

The Original Objectives

The Benefaction knew from very early on that they wanted to have something tangible to give to the Boys And Girls Club at the end of the semester. It was one thing to try and simply raise money for the club, but we felt it was far better to find something they needed, and get it done for them.

So to figure out what the club might need, and whether or not we could provide it, we took a trip to the club. We found out that there were quite a few things the club needed that we could handle.

For starters, they needed lots of IT related work done on the clubs computer labs. We quickly signed up for that responsibility; having two computer science majors and two It majors meant we were perfect for that task.

As for something tangible, Jenny gave us a list of ideas. One was a security suite called K9 that would help keep the kids safe online. Another was a lobby televesion that looped through a presentation. We decided to start raising money with the intention of providing one of these two things to the club.

Change Of Plans

During the process of upgrading the computer labs, we had the chance to talk to Jenny again about purchase ideas. We had our funding from the candy sales, and that made it a lot easier to find a clear directive.

First, we ruled the K9 security idea out. The upgrades we did on the machine included ehanced security, and Jenny didn't seem as concerned anymore about purchasing a licence for K9.

Then Jenny heard that Tyler L worked at a local print shop in town. She wondered if we might be able to convince them to design and print a stand-up banner that she could use at conventions to identify the club's table.

We drew up a new gantt chart, and reviewed our funds, and everything was shifted towards the banner.

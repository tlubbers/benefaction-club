BGC Banner

Status: Completed


Project Story

A Change of Direction

During our IT upgrade day, The Benefaction had a chance to re-evaluate our progress with Jenny at the club. After setting up the latest version of Windows on all of the computer systems, each system had Windows Defender security features configured, and the need for K-9 Security was no longer a priority for the club.

None the less, we still had the money from our candy sales, and we were hoping to use it for something tangible that the club could use. Jenny heard that Tyler worked at The Copy Shop, a local print shop, and asked us if we would be interested in using the candy sales money for a stand-up banner that they could use at non-for-profit conventions.

Thanks Copy Shop!

Tyler was able to speak to the owner of The Copy Shop, and work out a discount on deisgn and print services, bringing the total cost within our price-range. We saved the club over $270! Thanks very much Copy Shop!

Getting It Done

Working directly with Alicia at the club, Tyler was able to put together the design and get the banner printed. The club loved it.

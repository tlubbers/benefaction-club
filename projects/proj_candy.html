Candy Sales

Status: Completed


Project Story

Finding Funding

The Benefaction knew from very early on that they wanted to have something tangible to give to the Boys And Girls Club at the end of the semester. It was one thing to try and simply raise money for the club, but we felt it was far better to find something they needed, and get it done for them.

Of course, that meant we were going to need money. Once we found out that we couldn't use causevox funds for our tangible projects, we decided to jump on the candy sales bandwagon.

Conflicting Minds

It turns out, there is a about a million ways to sell candy. We spent a long time trying to come to a consensus on how to go about selling the sugary goods. Some of us felt we should sell a lot for little cost, and some of us felt we should sell a little for a lot. Some of us wanted to sell full size brand name candy bars, and some of us wanted to sell fun sized generics. We were clearly still in the storming stages of group development.

Consensus

Tim and Tyler P came up with an idea that finally brought the group to a consensus. They proposed we buy a variety of miniature sized candy in bulk, and then package them up into pretty little collections of tasty treasures. This allowed us to sell them each at an increased price, without spending to much money. Great idea, right?!

Results

The candy sales went great! Every package we made was sold, and we received the funding we needed to pursue one our tangible project idea. For exact numbers, check our financials in the "more" section in the navigation menu!
